WHY US?


Greetings from IEEE-SSIT family!

Want to earn an opportunity to enhance your technical, managerial and designing skills? This is just the right place for you! We will be opening our doors for recruitments on 11th December, 2019 [Wednesday] from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM @ SJT521/522. We are looking for Passionate, dedicated and hard working people to turn up for the auditions. We hope that your selection takes you to new heights and streamlines and polishes your abilities and areas of interests.
IEEE Society on Social Implications of Technology is concerned with how technology impacts the world, and how its applications can change the world for better. The VIT bureau of IEEE-SSIT was formed on January 10th, 2017 and since then we have taken up quality projects in various streams of engineering, organized multiple workshops, hosted innumerable events and left an indelible mark in the competitive landscape.  From Open source to Hack4Cause, we've proved to be one of the most active chapters in the campus, covering not only diverse concepts but diving into trending domains of technology as well.
Now it's time for you to show how you are better than the rest. Do not miss the chance to be a part of one of the most promising chapters in VIT.
